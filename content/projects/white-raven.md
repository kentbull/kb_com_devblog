An architecture for humane payment channels connecting to the modern central banking system. White Raven explores EUTxO-based reference implementations for CBDC payment channels, drawing on concepts from Bitcoin Lightning Network, Ethereum, and programmable money. A research-oriented platform for building privacy-preserving digital currency infrastructure.
