# What makes KERI different: Every User an Identity Provider

Dr. Smith clarifies [here](https://github.com/WebOfTrust/keripy/issues/672#issuecomment-1942994524) that every user is an identity provider.

Writing notes:Show why and how this is different from traditional, legacy identity systems.

Bitcoin and crypto started popularizing the idea that you control your identity. This is the logical conclusion and evolution of that.

> No bearer tokens, no ephemeral tokens, no trusted third party identity providers. Each AID controller is its own identity provider. Dr. Samuel Smith
