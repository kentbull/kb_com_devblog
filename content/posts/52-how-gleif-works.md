# How GLEIF Works


GLEIF provides NIL-2 identity assurance in a cryptographically verifiable format. That is the value GLEIF provides. This is cryptographically verifiable up to the GLEIF Root of Trust.

Percholated Discovery is how TELs (registry, issuer, and usage) and KELs are discovered. ACDCs are presented.
